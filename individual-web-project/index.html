    Exploring Cake Poppin'

    How It All Began

    Cake Poppin' is a recently created small business based out of Little Rock Arkansa. Its Creator Ciera Prunty started her business December 27 2019. Cake Poppin' is a bakery that specializes in Cakes pops, dipped strawberries, and custom cakes. The more we grow the better we improve. We make sure we have the best quality products for our consumers.
